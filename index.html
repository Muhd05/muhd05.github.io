Learning Reflection

I learned that Informatics Media combines technology, design, communication, and user experience. A good digital product must not only function correctly, but must also be usable, attractive, accessible, and safe.

Project work improved my ability to plan, design, test, and refine digital artefacts. It also helped me connect classroom theories with practical design and development tasks.

Digital media platforms collect, display, and distribute information. Therefore, media technologists need basic awareness of authentication, privacy, safe communication, responsible content sharing, and secure web practices.

I plan to improve JavaScript, UI/UX testing, secure web development, content strategy, and professional project documentation.
